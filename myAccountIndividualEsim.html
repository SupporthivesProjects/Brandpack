An eSIM is a digital SIM built directly into your phone, allowing you to install mobile data instantly without inserting anything physical. Instead of swapping SIM cards, you simply download your plan and connect. It’s faster, more secure, and designed for modern travel, giving you immediate access to data wherever you go.

You can install your eSIM by scanning the QR code provided after purchase.

Install it before your trip so it activates when you reach your destination.
